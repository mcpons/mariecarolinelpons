Western Magar Language

During my Masters degree, I have conducted research on the Western variety of Magar spoken in the district of Syangja.

I wrote a Grammar Sketch of the language for my Masters' thesis.

I have collected oral literature with Magar speakers from several villages in the areas of Nibuwakharka and Jagatradevi and analysed the language structures in collaboration with Maya Rana and Deepa Rana.
